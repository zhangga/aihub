# Source Playbook

Use this file to gather and balance social-first signals across the default platform set.

## Default Platform Set

- Xiaohongshu
- Weibo
- WeChat official accounts
- X or Twitter

## What Each Platform Is Good At

### Xiaohongshu

Best for:

- everyday life use cases
- creator packaging styles
- emotion-heavy reactions
- "is this actually useful?" discussions

Look for:

- repeated note formats
- checklist or tutorial framing
- comments revealing aspiration, anxiety, or imitation

### Weibo

Best for:

- sudden breakout attention
- public controversy
- celebrity, education, workplace, and social-value spillover

Look for:

- sharp peaks
- quote or repost velocity
- argument-driven framing rather than careful explanation

### WeChat Official Accounts

Best for:

- essays, explainers, and interpretation
- creator and media framing that may influence topic meetings
- stronger narratives than short-form social posts

Look for:

- repeated opinion framings
- synthesis articles that turn product updates into social meaning
- education and parenting commentary with higher detail

### X Or Twitter

Best for:

- upstream AI launch signals
- builder, researcher, and early adopter reactions
- the first wave of discussion before Chinese creator ecosystems digest it

Look for:

- original launch threads
- quote-post debates
- practical demos being copied or questioned

## Collection Strategy

Start broad, then tighten:

1. Build a candidate list from the last 3 days across all four platforms.
2. Group duplicates and near-duplicates into one topic.
3. Mark which platforms show the topic and how strong the signal is.
4. Pull only enough follow-up context to judge whether it is real and creator-relevant.

## Signal Patterns Worth Keeping

Prefer topics that show one or more of these patterns:

- cross-platform resonance
- obvious creator adaptation behavior
- discussion spilling from AI circles into everyday life circles
- unusually strong agreement or disagreement
- a practical tool or behavior people can copy immediately

## Signal Patterns To Distrust

Be careful with:

- single-post virality without follow-through
- rage bait with no durable content value
- hype based entirely on demos that few people can reproduce
- platform rumors that never reach a stable explanation

## Suggested Support Tools

If `sensight` is available:

- use `daily_social` for day-level discovery across the AI space
- use `social_search` for platform-specific follow-up inside the last 2 days
- use `search_events` for broader breakout context
- use `retrieve_summarize` only when you need a deeper article-level digest

If `sensight` is unavailable, gather manually and keep direct source links or names visible in the report.
